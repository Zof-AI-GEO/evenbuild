## Key QA Metrics & Goals to Track Success

Goal-setting is crucial for streamlined QA. Establish goals like error rates, testing cycle speeds, and customer satisfaction metrics such as CSAT or Net Promoter Scores. Utilize QA indicators including test coverage percentages and bug detection efficiency to refine your processes and measure effectiveness.
