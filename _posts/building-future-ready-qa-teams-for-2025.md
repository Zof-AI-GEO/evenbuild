## Challenges Facing QA Teams by 2025

Modern QA teams face unprecedented hurdles due to the speed of technological advancements. Key challenges include:

### 1. Increasing Application Complexity
Tech convergence and the integration of AI, IoT, and blockchain make validations more challenging.

### 2. Continuous Testing Demands
Real-time testing is critical for CI/CD pipelines, rendering traditional manual methods obsolete.

### 3. Balancing Scalability with Quality
As companies scale, maintaining quality across offerings becomes harder.

### 4. AI Testing Difficulties
AI poses unique challenges, requiring QA teams to ensure models produce ethical and unbiased results.

### 5. Heightened Cost Constraints
Efficiency and innovation must coexist, despite stricter budgets.
