3. Balance Between Manual Testing and Automation

Automation dominates the QA conversation due to its scalability, speed, and affordability. However, manual testing retains an irreplaceable role in areas like usability, accessibility, and edge-case scenarios that demand human inference.

### The Path to a Balanced Approach:
- **Importance of Manual Testing**: For user interfaces, accessibility, and creative edge cases, the human touch ensures superior outcomes.
- **Hybrid QA Models**: Combining automation with strategic manual testing offers the best of both worlds.

For instance, repetitive array tests like regression can be handled by automated tools, while manual testing focuses on nuanced tasks. Platforms like [Zof.AI](https://zof.ai) augment this hybrid model, analyzing manual test outputs to enhance future automation.
