## Key Transformations in QA Practices

Over the past decade, QA has shifted from siloed manual testing approaches to integrated, automated workflows embedded within DevOps pipelines. Between 2015 and 2023, practices like shift-left testing, model-based testing, and CI/CD integration brought agility, reliability, and seamless user experiences to the forefront. By 2025, QA will embrace intelligence-driven processes powered by AI, enabling predictive failure identification, self-healing systems, and scalable infrastructures for decentralized and complex applications.

### Blockchain QA
Blockchain testing frameworks will enforce decentralized architecture validation and compliance for regulated industries.

### VR/AR Testing
QA for immersive 3D environments will include latency checks, rendering validations, and multi-device compatibility.

### Cloud-native Frameworks
Cloud-based tools facilitate geographically distributed testing and scalable processes, enhancing QA efficiency in microservices-driven systems.
