## The Expanding Role of AI in Quality Assurance

AI's integration into QA has evolved significantly. From manual testing to script-based automation and now intelligent systems, QA processes continuously adapt to technological progress. Manual testing was not only laborious but also prone to error. While traditional automation accelerated testing, it depended heavily on static, predefined scripts, which often lacked adaptability.

AI has redefined this process entirely. Machine Learning (ML) processes massive datasets, uncovering errors with remarkable precision, while Natural Language Processing (NLP) enables automated complex interface tests. AI systems now simulate real-world user actions, predict flaws, and enhance efficiency. By 2025, platforms like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai) are driving smarter, predictive systems that enhance bug detection, optimize workflows, and deliver actionable insights faster than ever.
