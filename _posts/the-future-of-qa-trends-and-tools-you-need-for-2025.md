## Introduction to QA Evolution

Traditionally, QA ensured bug-free software before release, but modern practices like Agile and DevOps have embedded testing throughout development. By 2025, QA professionals will actively participate as strategists, leveraging automated tools and data analytics to prevent issues proactively. QA transitions into a predictive and tech-driven discipline, demanding innovation to stay ahead.
