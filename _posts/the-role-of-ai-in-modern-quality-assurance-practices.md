## How Quality Assurance Has Evolved in Software Development

Quality Assurance has played a key role in software development since its inception, ensuring seamless functionality and usability for consumers. In the beginning, QA relied heavily on manual testing, where testers executed test cases and manually reviewed code to find bugs. Although effective to some extent, manual processes were slow and prone to human error, especially in scaling operations.

Later, the transition to automated testing improved things significantly. Automated tools replaced repetitive manual tasks, delivering faster results and enabling teams to focus on more complicated scenarios. However, these early automation frameworks were still dependent on static rules and repetitive patterns, limiting their scalability.

With modern-day applications growing increasingly dynamic and complex, a more advanced approach to QA has become necessary. This is where AI has stepped in. By learning and adapting, AI is setting a new benchmark for smarter, quicker, and more accurate testing practices.
