Advantages and Challenges of AI in QA

AI offers:
- Enhanced accuracy by minimizing human error.
- Faster development timelines, crucial for agile and DevOps teams.
- Cost efficiency through reduced manual effort.
- Actionable analytics that offer deeper insights.
- Scalability for evolving business needs.

**Challenges to Expect**:
- Initial setup costs and steep learning curves.
- Trust-building in predicted results.
- Data security concerns during AI processing.
- Need for high-quality data as input.
