In 2025, **continuous testing** will become essential. Unlike traditional QA, which isolates testing phases, continuous testing integrates it across the software lifecycle. Benefits include:

1. **Real-Time Error Detection**
   Issues are resolved promptly during development, minimizing future defect backlogs.

2. **Improved Team Collaboration**
   QA and development teams collaborate seamlessly to resolve concerns and drive better outcomes.

3. **Faster Time-to-Market**
   Eliminating isolated QA delays accelerates deliveries, ensuring robust products reach users on time.
