Techniques Transforming QA

Modern practices are crucial as QA enters an era of more complex ecosystems:

### 1. **Shift-Left Testing**
Involving QA early in development reduces both costs and time-to-market.

### 2. **Test-Driven Development (TDD)**
Writing test cases before coding encourages high-quality results upfront.

### 3. **Exploratory QA**
Dynamic interaction combined with AI insights keeps human intuition pivotal in detecting software flaws.

### 4. **Performance and Chaos Testing**
Testing stress scenarios ensures robust systems in unpredictable environments.
