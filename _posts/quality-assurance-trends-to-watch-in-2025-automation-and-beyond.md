Tackling QA Challenges in IoT and Edge Computing

The rise of IoT and edge computing demands innovative QA strategies. IoT involves testing heterogeneous devices, while edge computing focuses on decentralized data processing, adding challenges to traditional QA workflows.

### IoT-Specific QA Needs
- **Device Compatibility**: Testing across diverse devices and protocols.
- **Security Measures**: Ensuring data privacy and safeguarding against vulnerabilities.
- **Stress Testing**: Managing thousands of concurrent connections.

### Edge Computing QA Strategies
- **Localized Testing**: Running simulations for edge environments.
- **Latency Checks**: Prioritizing ultra-low latency requirements.
- **System Resilience Validation**: Ensuring smooth failovers during interruptions.
