## What is Continuous Testing?

Continuous Testing (CT) integrates testing at every phase of software development, ensuring consistent quality checks from the initial stages to the final release. Unlike traditional QA methods that concentrate only on post-development testing, CT automates testing at scale, minimizing time and human effort while bolstering software quality. 

**Core CT Questions:**
- Is functionality reliable?
- Does the software withstand stress testing?
- Is the performance consistent across diverse environments?
