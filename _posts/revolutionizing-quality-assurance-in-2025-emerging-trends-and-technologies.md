Growth in IoT adoption requires QA teams to ensure reliability across billions of connected devices. By 2025, QA prioritizes:

- **Scalable Testing Environments**: Simulating real-world scenarios across massive device networks.
- **Security-Centric QA**: Addressing privacy and compliance for sensitive data processed by IoT systems.
- **Predictive Maintenance Testing**: Using platforms like Zof AI to identify and preempt failures in IoT networks.
