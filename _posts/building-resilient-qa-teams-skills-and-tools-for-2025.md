## Top Challenges for QA Teams in 2025

### High-Speed Development Cycles
As Agile and DevOps methodologies become the norm, QA teams must validate software at unprecedented speeds.

### Testing AI-Driven Systems
QA professionals will need expertise in verifying AI and machine learning models, including bias detection and predictive analysis.

### Complex Ecosystems
Seamlessly test growing networks of IoT devices, edge computing systems, and multiplatform environments.

### Security and Compliance
Stay ahead of ever-evolving cyber threats while adhering to global regulatory standards.
