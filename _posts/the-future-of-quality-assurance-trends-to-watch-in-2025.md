## How Quality Assurance Has Evolved Over Time

QA started as a manual, error-prone process. With the advent of agile and DevOps, QA became more integrated and streamlined, bolstered by automation. The early 2020s introduced AI and machine learning to QA, enabling predictive testing and advanced analytics. By 2025, these technologies promise to redefine QA roles and increase efficiency radically.
