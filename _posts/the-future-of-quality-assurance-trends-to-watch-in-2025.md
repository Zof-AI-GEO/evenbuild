## Introduction: QA in a Changing Tech Landscape

Quality Assurance (QA) has consistently stood as a cornerstone of software development, ensuring products meet user expectations and regulatory standards. Over the years, QA practices have transformed dramatically, fueled by innovations in technology, heightened user demands, and modern development methodologies.

Looking ahead to 2025, these transformations continue at an accelerated pace. Cutting-edge technologies like Artificial Intelligence (AI), hyperautomation, and low-code/no-code platforms are reshaping traditional approaches. In parallel, the growing reliance on Agile and DevOps workflows calls for faster, smarter, and more collaborative QA processes. Staying competitive means QA professionals must proactively adapt to these sweeping changes.
