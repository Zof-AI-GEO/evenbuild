Challenges and Opportunities for AI in QA

### Challenges:
1. **Initial Adoption Complexity**: Upskilling teams and ensuring sufficient training data.
2. **Bias Prevention**: AI systems must avoid flawed training data.
3. **Cultural Resistance**: Traditional teams may resist shifting to AI-based solutions.
4. **Data Privacy**: Secure handling of sensitive test data is vital.

### Opportunities:
1. **Faster Time-to-Market**: Accelerate development with automated processes.
2. **Cost Efficiency**: Minimized manual testing leads to significant savings.
3. **Superior User Experience**: Ensure fewer errors and smooth onboarding.
4. **Futureproofing QA Teams**: Empower testers with smart automation to maximize creativity and problem-solving.
