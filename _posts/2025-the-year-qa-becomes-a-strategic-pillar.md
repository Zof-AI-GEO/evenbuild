## The Critical Role of QA in 2025

As software grows more interconnected and complex, QA has shifted from a final development step to a continuous, proactive process. With AI, IoT, and personalized systems at the forefront, a single software failure can jeopardize user trust and a business's reputation. QA in 2025 guarantees performance, security, compliance, and accessibility at every phase of software development.
