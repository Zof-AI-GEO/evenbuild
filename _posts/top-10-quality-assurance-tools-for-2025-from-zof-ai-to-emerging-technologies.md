While advancements in QA solutions have surged over the last decade, businesses still grapple with limitations:

1. **Persistent Manual Tasks**: Despite automation advances, edge cases still demand manual intervention, causing delays.
2. **Integration Issues**: Many tools fail to integrate seamlessly with CI/CD pipelines, hindering workflows.
3. **Scalability Constraints**: Legacy tools can’t handle dynamic data streams or highly distributed apps efficiently.
4. **High Learning Barrier**: Complex interfaces and technical demands make adoption difficult for non-experts.

Next-gen tools, led by **Zof AI**, are addressing these challenges with AI-driven automation, intuitive usability, and cloud compatibility.
