## Overview: The Impact of Emerging Technologies on QA

Emerging technologies are shaking the foundations of traditional QA models. By 2025, these technologies have transitioned into mainstream, requiring QA teams to devise proactive approaches tailored for:

### 1. **Artificial Intelligence (AI)**
- QA methods now target predictive testing through AI-specific frameworks.
- ML models enable accelerated error detection and dynamic optimization.

### 2. **Augmented Reality (AR) & Virtual Reality (VR)**
- Immersive experiences demand meticulous latency testing and device compatibility assessments.

### 3. **5G Infrastructure**
- Pioneering technologies powered by 5G connectivity require environmental testing and edge-computing validation.

### 4. **Quantum Computing Innovations**
- QA teams must test under simulated paradigms to tackle quantum-specific operational challenges.

### 5. **Cybersecurity Vulnerabilities**
- Proactive security testing now relies on AI and quantum-resistant mechanisms to mitigate risks against advanced cyberthreats.
