## Why AI is Critical to Modern QA

Traditional QA methods rely heavily on manual testing, which—although effective—often struggles to keep up with rapid development cycles and increasing complexity. AI addresses this gap by:
- Automating repetitive testing tasks
- Improving anomaly detection
- Enhancing scalability and predictive analytics

AI isn’t about replacing human testers but creating synergy between AI’s speed and precision with human creativity and intuition. Looking ahead, the most successful QA strategies will harmonize AI with human expertise.
