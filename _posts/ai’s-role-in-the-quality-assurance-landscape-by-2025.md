Challenges of Adopting AI in QA 

Although AI’s potential in QA seems limitless, adoption faces several challenges:

1. **Initial Setup Costs**: Integrating solutions like Zof AI demands upfront investments in infrastructure and training.
2. **Skillset Gaps**: QA professionals need new skills in machine learning and AI tools.
3. **Cultural Resistance**: Traditional QA teams may struggle to adapt to AI-driven workflows.
4. **Data Privacy Concerns**: Advanced AI models require extensive data inputs, raising questions about privacy and compliance.
5. **Over-Reliance on AI**: Total dependence on AI tools may cause oversight of edge cases requiring human expertise.

Addressing these challenges requires fostering technical adaptability, bridging knowledge gaps, and ensuring ethical data usage.
