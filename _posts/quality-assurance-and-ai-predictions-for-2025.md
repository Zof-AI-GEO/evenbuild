## How AI is Revolutionizing QA

AI is shifting QA from static, manual testing to proactive solutions. Here are four ways AI enhances QA:

1. **Automated Test Creation**  
AI minimizes human error by autonomously generating test cases, aligning them with system requirements.

2. **Predictive Analysis**  
AI evaluates past data and user behavior to foresee system issues before they arise.

3. **Continuous Real-Time Monitoring**  
AI software tests systems continuously, providing instant alerts on vulnerabilities.

4. **Cross-Platform Testing**  
AI tools like Zof AI deliver seamless application testing across multiple devices and platforms.
