## Top Challenges QA Teams Will Face by 2025

New trends in the tech world will directly impact QA workflows. Here's a closer look at key challenges:

1. **Emerging Technologies**:
   Novel innovations like AI, blockchain, quantum computing, and IoT create intricate systems QA teams must validate to ensure interoperability and performance. 

2. **Faster Scalability Demands**:
   Enterprises will seek quicker scaling options for larger audiences. QA professionals must prioritize robust testing to maintain reliability under strain.

3. **Continuous Deployment Workflows**:
   With the rise of CI/CD pipelines, QA must seamlessly integrate into rapid, iterative workflows without compromising on quality.

4. **Increasing Cybersecurity Threats**:
   As cyberattacks grow sophisticated, QA methods must embed advanced security testing early in development, especially for AI models and cloud-based systems.

5. **Global Collaboration Challenges**:
   Remote, globally distributed teams will become the standard. QA practices need to focus on aligning diverse teams across locations and cultures.
