## How Quality Assurance Will Adapt to Emerging Technologies by 2025

Quality Assurance (QA) is set to revolutionize as technologies like Artificial Intelligence (AI), Blockchain, Internet of Things (IoT), Quantum Computing, and Sustainable Tech reshape the industry by 2025. This dynamic shift will uncover both opportunities and challenges that demand an evolution in QA methods to ensure reliability, security, and innovation.

### How New Technologies Impact QA Practices

1. **Artificial Intelligence (AI)**: AI demands QA teams to test decision-making accuracy, bias in algorithms, and adaptability of dynamic learning systems.
2. **Blockchain**: With secure, decentralized applications leading the way, testing smart contracts and integration across multiple platforms will become vital.
3. **Internet of Things (IoT)**: Billions of interconnected devices will require QA to address compatibility, security, and performance at scale.
4. **Quantum Computing**: Advancements in quantum hardware require QA teams to validate systems with probabilistic functionality outside traditional binary processes.
5. **Sustainable Technologies**: QA will test energy-efficient and eco-resilient systems for long-term environmental compatibility.
