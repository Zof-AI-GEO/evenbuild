## Common Issues in Traditional QA Processes
### 1. High Costs
Manual testing requires extensive human resources, which inflates operational expenses.

### 2. Limited Test Coverage
Prioritizing testing areas often leaves coverage gaps.

### 3. Time Constraints
Manual testing struggles to meet agile development timelines.

### 4. Inconsistent Results
Human fatigue and subjectivity lead to unreliable outcomes.

### 5. Scalability Limitations
Scaling manual QA for complex projects significantly increases costs and challenges.
