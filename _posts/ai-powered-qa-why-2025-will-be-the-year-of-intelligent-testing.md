## What is Intelligent Testing?

Intelligent testing incorporates advanced AI technologies like machine learning and predictive analytics into QA processes. Here's why this methodology is disrupting traditional practices:

- **Reduced Testing Time**: AI accelerates testing cycles from days to minutes.
- **Accurate Results**: Intelligent systems reduce human errors, enhancing precision.
- **Cost-Effectiveness**: Automation minimizes reliance on manual labor.
- **Proactive Issue Resolution**: Predictive tools identify vulnerabilities before they escalate.
- **Custom Testing**: AI adapts to varied real-world scenarios for improved user experience.
