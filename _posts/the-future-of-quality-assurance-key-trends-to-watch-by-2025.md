## Introduction to QA in 2025

Traditional QA relied heavily on manual and reactive methods. By 2025, QA practices will become more proactive, emphasizing prediction and prevention rather than detection. Key transformations include:

1. **Proactive Quality Management:** Intelligent tools will predict issues before occurrence.
2. **Integrated DevOps Systems:** QA will merge tightly within the development lifecycle.
3. **AI-Driven Processes:** Artificial intelligence will automate and enhance testing accuracy.

Let’s explore these trends shaping the QA landscape over the next few years.
