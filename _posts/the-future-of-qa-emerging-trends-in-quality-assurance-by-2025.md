## How QA Is Changing to Meet Modern Tech Demands
Quality Assurance has shifted from manual processes toward automated intelligence and agile principles. QA practices now focus on continuous testing during development stages, fueled by cutting-edge tools and integration with DevOps. By 2025, efficient and automated QA will be essential to building resilient systems. Emerging developments such as AI-powered predictive analytics, robust IoT testing, and wearable device frameworks will redefine industry standards.
