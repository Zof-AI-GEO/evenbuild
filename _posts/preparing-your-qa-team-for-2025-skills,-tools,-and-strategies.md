## Evaluating Your QA Team’s Current Skillsets for Future Readiness

Assessing your team's current competencies is key to preparing for tomorrow’s challenges. Focus on these three areas:

- **Technical Skills**: Are they skilled in modern programming languages, testing frameworks, and tech stacks like Kubernetes or Docker?
- **Adaptability**: Can they adapt quickly to new workflows, tools, or methodologies?
- **Strategic Thinking**: Are they capable of constructing robust test plans that address functionality, performance, and security?

These insights enable organizations to close gaps by implementing tailored professional development.
