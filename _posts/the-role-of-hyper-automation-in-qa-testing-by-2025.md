1. **AI-Driven Test Modeling**: QA teams increasingly leverage AI algorithms for testing, predicting system behavior, and automating complex scenarios.

3. **Self-Healing Mechanisms**: Hyper-automation platforms proactively identify and fix errors, minimizing downtime.
4. **Strategic Analytics**: Enhanced AI-driven reporting transforms test results into actionable business insights, ensuring optimized workflows.
