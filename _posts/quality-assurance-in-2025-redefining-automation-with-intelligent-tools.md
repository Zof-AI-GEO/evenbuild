Metrics That Matter for Intelligent QA in 2025

Performance and success in QA automation hinge on clear insights. Track these metrics to measure your results and optimize processes:

1. **Test Coverage Percentage**: Ensure comprehensive testing of application functionality, especially with cutting-edge automation tools.
2. **Defect Detection Rate**: Measure defects identified in testing versus those found in production.
3. **Automation ROI**: Analyze time, cost, and resource savings due to automation.
4. **Mean Time to Resolution (MTTR)**: Track how quickly issues are identified and resolved.
5. **Test Speed**: Measure testing time to maintain DevOps efficiency.
6. **False Positive/Negative Rates**: Assess AI accuracy in identifying valid defects.
