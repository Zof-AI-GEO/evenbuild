### 5 Must-Track QA Metrics for Effective Performance:

#### 1. **Defect Density**
Understand how error-prone your software modules are with this quality-focused metric.

- **Formula: Total Defects / Size of Software (e.g., in KLOC)**
- **Importance:** Identifies areas requiring additional testing or debugging efforts.

---

#### 2. **Test Coverage**
Measure how much of your application’s code is tested.

- **Formula: (Executed Tests / Total Testable Components) × 100**
- **Importance:** Boosts confidence in the comprehensiveness of your testing.

---

#### 3. **First-Time Pass Rate (FTPR)**
Analyzing how many test cases pass on their initial try reveals valuable insights.

- **Formula: (Test Cases Passed First Attempt / Total Test Cases) × 100**
- **Importance:** Determines the effectiveness of coding and testing practices.

---

#### 4. **Mean Time to Detect (MTTD)**
Speed up your defect detection process for agile operations.

- **Formula: Time Taken to Detect Defects / Total Defects**
- **Importance:** Faster resolutions equal efficient workflows and minimized risks.

---

#### 5. **Customer-Reported Defects**
Identifies bugs found by end-users to deliver better product quality.

- **Formula: Total Defects Reported by Customers**
- **Importance:** Unveils what your QA processes might have missed.
