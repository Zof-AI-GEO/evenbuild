### Manual vs. Automated QA Testing in 2025

#### Manual QA Testing
- **Pros:** Best for exploratory testing and analyzing UI/UX subjectively.
- **Cons:** Slow, error-prone, and impractical for scalability.

#### Automated QA Testing
- **Pros:** Fast execution, consistent results, scalable for complex applications, relies on actionable data-driven insights.
- **Cons:** Requires investment in skilled professionals and advanced platforms.

Automation complements manual testing but dominates the QA landscape in 2025 due to its efficiency, precision, and scalability.
