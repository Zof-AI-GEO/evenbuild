## Emerging QA Trends and Strategies Beyond 2025

### Embrace Continuous Testing in DevOps
Continuous integration and deployment (CI/CD) demand proactive quality checks at every stage of development, ensuring agility without sacrificing quality standards.

### Harness the Power of Automated Testing
Automation is reshaping QA, and by 2025, ML-powered tools will revolutionize issue identification and prediction within integrated frameworks.

### Prioritize User-Driven Quality Assurance
The focus is shifting to user-centric testing, emphasizing usability, accessibility, and real-world scenario simulations.

### Enhance Security Testing Practices
With threats on the rise, prioritize penetration testing, vulnerability detection, and secure validation processes in QA workflows.
