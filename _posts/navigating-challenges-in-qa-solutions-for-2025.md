## Key Challenges for QA in 2025

### Increasing Complexity of Systems and Applications
Modern technologies' integration creates multifaceted ecosystems demanding rigorous testing across diverse compatibility challenges. 

### Pressure for Faster Delivery Timelines
QA cycles compressed by agile development and CI/CD pipelines require automation to preserve test integrity.

### AI Bias Testing and Ethics Verification
QA professionals must ensure fairness, ethical compliance, and transparency in AI-driven systems.

### Shrinking Skilled Workforce Supply
Skill gaps related to automation frameworks, performance testing, and AI knowledge hinder QA operations.

### Regulatory Compliance Evolution
Adherence to regulations like GDPR, HIPAA, CCPA, and emerging AI standards is critical.
