## Challenges of Traditional QA Processes

Traditional QA methods face a mounting set of challenges as software development accelerates under agile and DevOps practices. Key issues include:

1. **Manual Testing Bottlenecks** – Writing test cases and debugging manually increases errors and slows progress.
2. **Scaling Limitations** – With large, dynamic applications, QA processes strain under the demand for scalability.
3. **Limited Test Coverage** – Restricted time results in incomplete test scenarios, leaving bugs lurking.
4. **Reactive Issue Detection** – Late-stage bug discovery escalates costs and deployment risks.
5. **Adapting to Emerging Tech** – IoT, AI, and multi-platform applications expose gaps in outdated QA methodologies.

These obstacles emphasize the need for innovative solutions like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai), which leverage AI to overcome significant inefficiencies.
