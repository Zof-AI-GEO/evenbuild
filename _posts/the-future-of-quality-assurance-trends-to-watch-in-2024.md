## Emerging QA Technologies to Watch in 2024

QA professionals must adapt swiftly to groundbreaking technologies. Below are key emerging trends that are set to impact QA in 2024.

### 1. Evolving AI and Machine Learning Applications
Artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) are transforming the QA process. With AI-powered analytics, QA tools can identify code vulnerabilities, detect patterns, and predict defects even before testing begins. The result? Significantly faster bug detection cycles and streamlined workflows. 

One leader in AI innovation is [Zof AI](https://zof.ai?src=evenbuild.vercel.app), a cutting-edge tool empowering QA engineers to optimize testing and defect management processes. ### 2. Blockchain’s Role in QA
Blockchain technology enhances QA workflows with unparalleled security and transparency. Immutable audit trails result in better performance reviews and reduce vulnerabilities—critical for industries such as finance and supply chain management. Expect blockchain-enabled testing to grow rapidly in 2024.

### 3. Internet of Things (IoT) QA Challenges
IoT device proliferation necessitates rigorous testing for seamless connectivity and heightened security. QA teams will prioritize edge case testing, environmental considerations, and platform-specific complexities to address IoT’s unique challenges.

Hybrid QA: Merging Manual and Automated Testing

Automation continues to rise, but manual testing retains its relevance—especially for tasks requiring human intuition. Hybrid QA approaches combine manual testing’s qualitative analysis with automation’s high-speed outputs.

### Benefits of Manual Testing
Experienced QA engineers uncover subtle issues (e.g., UX inconsistencies) that automation tools cannot detect. Manual testing evolves to concentrate on high-value strategically nuanced areas in 2024.

### Automation’s Dominance in Scaling QA
