## What is Automated QA Testing?

Automated QA testing uses specialized tools and pre-written scripts to test applications. Instead of relying on manual efforts, automation ensures efficiency, consistency, and cost-effective scalability. Benefits of automation include:

- **Speed**: Shorten lengthy testing cycles with automated scripts.
- **Consistency**: Eliminate human errors for accurate results.
- **Growth Flexibility**: Scale testing efforts without limitations.
- **Savings**: Reduce costs over time by minimizing manual labor.

Popular frameworks like Selenium and Cypress paved the way for advanced automation tools like Zof AI that leverage Artificial Intelligence (AI) for optimized QA processes.
