## Emerging Technologies Reshaping QA Practices

Emerging technologies are revolutionizing software systems, demanding robust QA processes. Key areas of impact include:

1. **AI and ML**: QA professionals must validate AI-driven decision-making systems for data accuracy, fairness, and ethics.
2. **IoT**: Devices require rigorous testing to ensure hardware-software integration, security, and cross-device compatibility in real-world conditions.
3. **Blockchain**: Preparedness for auditing cryptographic security, ledger functionality, and scalability will be critical.
4. **Edge Computing**: Testing for latency, synchronization, and real-time performance gains prominence.

These advancements elevate QA beyond reactive measures, encouraging proactive strategies to ensure software resilience.
