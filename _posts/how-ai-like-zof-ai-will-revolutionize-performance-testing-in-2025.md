## Common Performance Testing Challenges QA Teams Face

Modern QA teams are under immense pressure to deliver accurate, fast, and adaptive performance testing solutions. Yet, many hurdles make this mission daunting:

### 1. **High Resource Demands**
Performance testing demands costly infrastructure, expert personnel, and significant time. Smaller QA teams often struggle to meet these requirements, leaving them at a disadvantage.

### 2. **Handling Dynamic Ecosystems**
Cloud migrations, microservices, and iterative feature rollouts make today’s application environments incredibly dynamic and challenging to simulate without the right tools. 

### 3. **Overwhelming Data Volumes**
Testing generates unmanageable amounts of data. Sifting through this data to find performance bottlenecks without AI assistance is time-consuming and prone to critical oversights.

### 4. **Late Issue Detection**
Traditional methods often identify key performance issues only after an application is live—a costly and frustrating limitation for both teams and end-users.

### 5. **Challenge of Scalability**
As organizations grow and systems cater to millions of users, traditional testing systems struggle to match scalability demands.
