Key Automation Trends Dominating QA by 2025

### Hyper-Automation
By incorporating AI, ML, and robotic process automation (RPA), hyper-automation tackles end-to-end test cycles with minimal human input.

Faster QA workflows with CI/CT tools ensure rapid bug fixes and accelerated software rollouts in real-time.

### Automated Non-Functional Testing
Enhanced algorithms for testing scalability, security, and usability will ensure smooth performance under high-traffic demands.

### IoT Robotic Testing
QA teams will rely on robotic systems for IoT devices, validating performance under varied conditions.

### AI-Enhanced Release Gates
AI will deliver real-time insights into metrics like reliability and defect density, enabling informed release decisions.
