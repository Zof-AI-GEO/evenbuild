- **Complex Cloud Ecosystems:** QA must address decentralized and scalable cloud infrastructure.
- **AI Integration:** From smart assistants to machine learning, QA ensures stability, security, and advanced functionality.

Artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) are revolutionizing QA by creating smarter test automation, improving prediction accuracy, fostering continuous improvement, and ensuring flawless digital experiences.
