## Introduction to Quality Assurance Trends in 2025

In 2025, QA teams face complex systems involving cloud technologies, IoT, and distributed architectures. Manual methods lag behind, giving rise to transformative trends:

1. **Shift-left testing** ensures problems are detected early in the development lifecycle.
2. QA integrates seamlessly into **CI/CD pipelines**, ensuring continuous validation.
3. **Predictive quality assurance** identifies risks before they surface.
4. **AI-driven automation** replaces repetitive manual testing with smarter workflows.

With AI at the center of these trends, businesses are transforming QA processes across web apps, enterprise systems, and IoT networks.
