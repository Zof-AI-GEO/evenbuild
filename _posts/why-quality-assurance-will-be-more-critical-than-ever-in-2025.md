## Why QA Matters More Than Ever

Modern software systems involve environments of APIs, microservices, cloud deployments, and vast lines of code. By 2025, these complexities will multiply with breakthroughs in AI, IoT, AR/VR, and blockchain—all creating a critical need for comprehensive QA strategies.

### Key QA Trends 

1. **Artificial Intelligence (AI):** QA ensures unbiased, ethical, and error-free AI systems that make real-time decisions across industries. 
2. **Internet of Things (IoT):** Billions of connected devices demand QA for seamless communication, dynamic environments, and robust security.
3. **Blockchain:** QA is crucial as blockchain systems require resilience due to irreversible operations.
4. **Augmented and Virtual Reality (AR/VR):** Delivering user-rich experiences requires bug-free environments optimized for immersion.
