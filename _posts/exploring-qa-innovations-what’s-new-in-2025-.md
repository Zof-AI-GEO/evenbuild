## Machine Learning: The Future of Defect Detection

Machine learning is enhancing defect detection by automatically analyzing patterns in historical data, predicting potential trouble areas, and even initiating self-healing mechanisms. This ensures better adaptability, accuracy, and overall efficiency.

### Key ML Innovations:
- **Pattern Recognition**: Identify recurring bugs and prevent them in real-time.
- **Zero-Day Issue Prediction**: Spot vulnerabilities traditional QA may miss.
- **Dynamic Upgrades**: Continuously improve with every dataset.
