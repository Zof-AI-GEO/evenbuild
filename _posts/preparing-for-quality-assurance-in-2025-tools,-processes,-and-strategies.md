## Evolving QA Landscape: Key Changes to Expect

The growth of advanced technologies—AI, machine learning, and DevOps—is shifting QA from manual efforts to streamlined automation. By 2025, QA teams must adapt to increasingly complex development demands, faster feedback loops, and sustainability goals.

### Transformative QA Practices and Trends

1. **AI and Automation**: Advanced tools predict bugs and optimize testing cycles.
2. **Continuous Testing**: Integrated testing workflows power rapid deployment.
3. **Sustainability**: Practices focused on green testing reduce resource consumption.
4. **Collaboration**: Shift-left/right testing ensures quality at every phase of development.

### Continuous Integration (CI) & Continuous Testing (CT)

### Collaborative QA Approaches

Cross-functional teamwork ensures alignment between QA, DevOps, design, and business goals. Shift-left and shift-right principles combined with tools like Trello and Slack drive efficiency.

### Responsible QA Practices

Sustainability in QA involves leveraging energy-efficient tools, optimizing test runs, and ensuring inclusivity with accessibility testing.
