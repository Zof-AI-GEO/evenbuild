## What is QA Automation and Why It's Critical in 2025?

Quality Assurance (QA) automation involves leveraging automated tools and processes to test software applications for defects, vulnerabilities, and performance issues. In 2025, QA automation is essential for adapting to complex software ecosystems, faster release cycles, and consumer demands for flawless digital experiences.
