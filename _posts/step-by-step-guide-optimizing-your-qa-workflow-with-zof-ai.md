## Rethinking Traditional QA Workflows

Traditional QA workflows rely heavily on manual processes, including:

### 1. Requirement Documentation
QA teams evaluate project needs and create test cases to meet defined specifications.

### 2. Test Design and Execution
This step encompasses manual or automated execution of test cases using conventional tools.

### 3. Bug Reporting
Identified bugs are documented with descriptions, reproduction steps, and fix suggestions.

### 4. Regression Testing
Updates undergo regression testing to ensure changes don’t disrupt existing features.

### 5. Final QA Validation
Successful testing concludes with QA sign-off for deployment readiness.

While essential, manual workflows often face hurdles like inefficiency, human error, and high costs. Let’s explore these challenges and how AI-powered solutions like Zof AI can address them.
