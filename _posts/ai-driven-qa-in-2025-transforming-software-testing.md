## What Is AI-driven QA?

AI-driven QA leverages artificial intelligence and machine learning to improve, automate, and quicken software testing processes. Traditional QA depended on manual testing and static automation techniques, which often posed challenges like inefficiency, human error, and slow adoption to dynamic development cycles.

AI-driven QA evolves this paradigm by introducing systems that adapt, learn, and continuously improve. AI algorithms simulate user behavior, identify defects, and assess intricate multi-platform solutions, excelling in cloud-native and hybrid environments. This approach ensures robust software with faster time-to-market, enhancing customer satisfaction and streamlining operations.
