## How QA is Redefining IoT and Smart Device Management

The rapid rise of IoT and smart ecosystems demands advanced QA practices to ensure reliability:

- **Interconnectivity Testing:** With over 75 billion IoT devices expected, seamless communication between systems and networks is critical. QA teams must design advanced strategies for secure, efficient communication protocols.
- **Security First:** Cybersecurity integration is a must at every phase, utilizing penetration testing and encryption validation to safeguard IoT systems.
- **Scalability Testing:** Validate performance under high traffic and network loads using scalable simulations that mimic real-world conditions.
