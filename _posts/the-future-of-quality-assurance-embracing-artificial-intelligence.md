### 3. Dynamic Self-Healing Automation
Automation frameworks powered by AI adapt seamlessly to software changes.

### 4. Enhanced Test Coverage
AI simulations expand testing scope, covering more complex usage scenarios.

### 5. Continuous Testing for Real-Time Insights
AI enables ongoing monitoring of application health even after deployment.
