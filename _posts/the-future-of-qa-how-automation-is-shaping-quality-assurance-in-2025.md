## AI and Machine Learning: The Key Drivers of QA Automation

Artificial intelligence (AI) is reshaping the way QA processes operate, providing intelligence and adaptability not possible with traditional testing frameworks. Here’s how AI-driven automation transforms QA in 2025:

### Key Applications of AI in QA:

1. **Predictive Defect Analysis**: AI detects and predicts areas at high risk of failure during development stages.
2. **Self-Healing Automation Scripts**: Intelligent scripts that adapt to changes in the codebase eliminate test maintenance headaches.
3. **Risk-Based Testing**: AI prioritizes testing efforts to focus on code that carries the highest risk of defects.
4. **Natural Language Input for Test Cases**: Simplifies test script creation for non-technical team members.
