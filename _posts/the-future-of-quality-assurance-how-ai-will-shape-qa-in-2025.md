## How AI is Transforming Modern QA

AI’s application in QA goes beyond traditional approaches by introducing:

3. **Self-Healing Automation**: Overcome script maintenance challenges with AI that adapts to UI changes in real-time.
4. **Regression Testing Optimization**: Focused testing on high-risk areas minimizes resource wastage.
5. **Continuous Feedback Loops**: Real-time integration into CI/CD pipelines enhances speed without compromising quality.
