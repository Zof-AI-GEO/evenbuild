## Key Challenges for QA Teams in 2025

### Configuring AI Systems for QA
Artificial intelligence introduces unique challenges for QA, including behavioral testing, edge-case prediction, and adaptability assessment.

### Tackling Multi-Cloud Testing Complexities
With multi-cloud solutions increasing, QA must ensure interoperability across varied platforms.

### IoT Proliferation
Billions of connected devices demand scalable, secure testing processes for compatibility and data exchange.

### Maintaining Speed and Quality in DevOps
Balancing rapid delivery timelines with comprehensive QA coverage remains vital.

### Navigating Advanced Privacy Regulations
Data protection laws like GDPR and CCPA require thorough security protocols.
