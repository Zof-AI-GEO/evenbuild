## The Challenges Facing QA Today

QA teams face several obstacles, including:

1. **Rising Complexity**: Ensuring compatibility across platforms and devices is becoming increasingly challenging.
2. **Limited Scalability**: Traditional methods can't keep pace with agile workflows.
3. **Human Errors**: Manual testing is prone to mistakes and resource constraints.
4. **Time-Consuming Regression Testing**: Managing regressions in fast-evolving software is laborious.
5. **Insufficient Coverage**: Constraints often limit the ability to test edge cases or vulnerabilities.

To overcome these hurdles, innovative AI solutions are emerging as a game changer.
