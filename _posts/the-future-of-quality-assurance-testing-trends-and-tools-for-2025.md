#### 1. Cloud-Based Testing on the Rise
The dominance of cloud platforms by 2025 ensures quick, scalable, and cost-efficient application testing under varied conditions.

Blockchain ensures trust, compliance, and transparent traceability in testing—vital for regulatory industries like finance and healthcare.

#### 3. IoT Ecosystem Testing
As IoT expands, QA testers will face intricate challenges in ensuring seamless connectivity and functional accuracy of connected devices.

#### 4. 5G: A New Frontier for QA
5G's rapid adoption highlights the need for high-speed testing environments, catering to lower latency and greater data flow.
